These files constitute supplemental material for J. Wilcox, C. Miller, T.L. Peters, and J.J. Bull:  Phage T7 adaptation to two antibiotics.  Files contour_noK_b.c and contour_noK_k.c are C programs that simulate ordinary differential equations for phage growth and cell growth and death.  Except for the ranges of parameter values considered, they are the same programs used in J J. Bull, G. Kaur and S.M. Krone:  Modeling the Phage Properties Best for Therapy. Viruses. 2026 Feb 13;18(2):240.
They use the Euler method with a step size of 0.01.  Parameter values requiring input are given in Wilcox et al.  Output files are processed by Figure 5.nb to generate Figure 5 in the Wilcox et al. paper.
